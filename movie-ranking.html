The best films according to Sasha

My top 3 movies of all time

The Picture of Dorian Gray

A film that left its mark on my soul as a teenager. Although, mostly, that little girl fell in love with Ben Barnes.

Cheaper by the Dozen

A film about a large family with eleven children. It's very funny and kind, reminds me of my childhood.

The Notebook

A film about great and strong love through the years and problems.
